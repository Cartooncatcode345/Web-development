Cuisines of bangladesh

Bangladeshi cuisine centers on rice, fish, and lentils (dal), with an emphasis on flavorful curries and vegetables

There are many types of cuisines we eat in our bangladesh.Its also an tradition for us to eat with our own hands.

Rice: The primary staple, forming the base of most meals
Fish Essential to the diet, with hundreds of varieties available, including carp, catfish, and the popular Hilsa fish.
Lentils (Dal) A common source of protein, often served as a curry or stew with rice.
Vegetables Eaten in various forms, including curries, fried dishes, and mashed preparations like begun bharta (mashed eggplant).
Briyani A flavorful rice dish cooked with meat (chicken, mutton, beef), fish, or vegetables and spices.
Kachchi Biryani A unique version often served during special occasions like weddings.
Panta Bhat A simple, refreshing dish of fermented cold rice, often eaten in the summer.
Pitha: Sweet and savory rice cakes popular during festivals and celebrations.
Chotpoti & Fuchka Popular street foods featuring spiced chickpeas and tangy tamarind water.
